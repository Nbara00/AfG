    AfG Alliance Rules

    0. Terms of subscription

    a. Discord required (just listening)

    (Click on the alliance and click on the chat group to connect.)

    b. Trial boss and people who can actively participate in the event

    c. A person who is well observing the rules and active in the growth of the alliance.

    d. If you are inactive for more than 3 days or you cannot log in, you can leave

    (If you are not connected for a long period of time, please tell the executives in advance.)

    e. If a coalition is needed, you can ignore the instructions or leave if you do not listen

    (Do not act alone)

    1. Collection Rules

    a. King's Landing (KL), Storm's End (SE) and Neutral Area

    (Be careful not to go to other areas, especially River Run and Eyrie. Attacked without warning.)

    b. When collecting, send troops to collect at once.

    If you leave resources, nothing new will happen. Don't leave resources.

    (If you attack what other allies have collected, you can leave resources and post a battle report.)

    c. The use of accelerated items while going to the collection area is prohibited.

    d. Level 5 gold coins can be collected from 1 to 2 by reaching level 25 of Maester's Tower.

    (If the collection time is not around 4 hours during level 25, increase the collection speed as soon as possible.)

    e. Except for level 5 gold coins, other level 5 resources can be freely collected.

    f. At level 25 of Maester's Tower, officers are notified and they are confirmed by recon.

    2. Rally Rules

    a. The Rebel Camp Rally City Scouting Report is posted in the Alliance Chat window.

    3. PO buff rule

    a. The PO buff is invited to the AfGbuff chat room after reaching level 20 of the castle.

    b. How to use PO is as follows.

    1) When PO ON is displayed in the chat room above

    2) Apply for the item you want to buff. (B+ for construction, r+ for research, t+ for training)

    3) Confirm that the applied buff is applied. (Instructions pass through the game screen or come in letters.)

    4) Click on the applied construction or research or training and click on help.

    5) Request to withdraw the buff applied to the chat room again. (B- for construction, r- for research, t- for training)

    c. Please prepare before applying for a buff. (Acceleration item, resource item)

    d. If you receive a buff, try to return it in 1-2 minutes.

    e. Only one buff can be applied at a time.

    (Buffs are given in the order in which they were applied. Please wait.)

    4. Rules for using Truce items

    a. If you don't want to be attacked by the Free Attack Day, the 6th Strongest Lord (Lord of War), or the KVK, be sure to use the truce item.

    5. Event Rules

    a. Try Trial bosses and Rebel Leaders every day if possible.

    (Rebel Leaders must be completely cleared before being recorded. The number of attacks is meaningless.)

    b. Alliance Conquest, Army of Dead gives gifts to individuals and allies.

    (If time matches, please schedule as much as possible to participate.)

    In the case of Alliance Conquest, be careful not to attack alone. Only scores are given to opponents.)

    c. Alliance Mobilization lasts about a week.

    The number of quests initially granted can be completed during the period, so you do not need to complete it in a short period of time.

    Get a quest that you can easily do among the highest possible quests. And if canceled, the count is deducted.

    6. Alliance Tier Rules

    a. Ⅰ-Secondary Character / Ⅱ-Main Character / Ⅲ-Maester's Tower 25LV

    (If you have any changes to your tier after checking your tier, please inform the executives.)

    7. Other

    a. Be sure to read the alliance letter and check each chat room frequently.

    b. Anyone who breaks the rules or harms the atmosphere of the alliance will be expelled.

    c. AfG exists to help each other grow. Therefore, I hope that you will have a concession.

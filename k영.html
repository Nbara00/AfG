    AfG Alliance Rules

    0. Terms of subscription

    a. Discord required (just listening)

    (Click on the alliance and click on the chat group to connect.)

    b. Daily active players, who participate in all Alliance Events

    c. Players who observe and obey the rules, and that are active in the growth of the alliance.

    d. If you are inactive for more than 3 days or you cannot log in, you might be expelled

    (If you are not connected for a long period of time, please tell the officers in advance.)

    e. The Alliance requires the contribution from each and every

    one of their members, do not act alone, don't ignore the instructions, or leave if you do not listen

    1. Collection Rules

    a. AfG owns King's Landing (KL) and Storm's End (SE), you can also gather in Neutral Area

    (Be careful not to gather on other areas, especially River Run and Eyrie, or you will be attacked without warning.)

    b. When collecting, be sure to send enough troops to collect at once. Do not leave nodes unfinished.

    If you leave nodes unfinished, new nodes will not appear.

    c. The use of marching speed ups while going to the collection area is prohibited.

    d. Level 5 gold mints can only be collected by players who own a level 25 of Maester's Tower, with a maximum of 2 at a time.

    (If the collection time is not around 4 hours during level 25, increase the collection speed as soon as possible.)

    e. Except for level 5 gold mints, other level 5 resource nodes can be freely collected.

    f. When your Maester's Tower reaches level 25, you shall notify Alliance officers, and they shall confirm and give recognition,

    in order for you to be enabled to gather level 5 gold mints.

    2. Rally Rules

    a. The Rebel Camp Rally City Scouting Report must be posted in the Alliance Chat window, before starting the Rally Attack.

    3. PO buff rule

    a. Once reaching Castle level 20, every player is invited to the AfG buff chat room, where you can access PO buff.

    b. How to use PO is as follows:

    1) PO ON message should be displayed in the chat room mentioned above

    2) Apply for the item you want to buff: (B+ for construction, R+ for research, T+ for training)

    3) Wait for confirmation that the buff you asked for is applied

    (a message should pass through the game screen, and a you should get a Raven mail stating "conferred title")

    4) Click on the applied buff (construction, research, or training), and after that, click on help

    5) As soon as you start your (construction, research, or training), request to withdraw the buff applied in the chat room again (B- for construction, R- fo

    c. Please prepare before applying for a buff (acceleration items, resources, talents, equipment)

    d. If you receive a buff, try to return it as soon as possible (1-2 minutes)

    e. Only one buff can be applied at a time (if you need two or more buffs, ask for one, withdraw it, and ask for another after that)

    f. Buffs are given in the order in which they were applied; please wait, if necessary

    4. Rules for using Truce items

    a. If you don't want to be attacked on the Free Attack Day (once every month),

    or the 6th Phase of Lord of Lords Event (Lord of War), or KVK, be sure to use the truce item.

    5. Event Rules

    a. Use your Trial Bosses and Rebel Leaders attempts every day, if possible.

    (Rebel Leaders must be completely cleared, in order to count for the Alliance record; if not cleared, the number of attacks is meaningless)

    b. Alliance Conquest and Army of Dead events give gifts to individuals and allies.

    (If time matches, please schedule as much as possible to participate)

    (In the case of Alliance Conquest, be careful not to attack alone, that only gives score to opponents)

    c. Alliance Mobilization event lasts about a week (8 days)

    (The number of quests initially granted can be completed during a given period of time, so you do not need to complete it inmediately)

    (Get a quest that you can easily do among the highest possible quests; if canceled, the quest count is deducted, so try not to cancel)

    6. Alliance Tier Rules

    a. Ⅰ- Knight: Alt Accounts / Ⅱ- Baron: Main Accounts / Ⅲ- Earl: Players with Maester's Tower 25LV

    (If you have any changes to your Tier, please inform the Alliance Officers)

    7. Other

    a. Be sure to read the all Alliance letters, and check each chat room periodically.

    b. Anyone who breaks the Alliance rules, or harms the Alliance environment, will be expelled.

    c. AfG exists to help each other grow, therefore, I hope that you will have a concession.
